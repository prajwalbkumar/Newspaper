View templates should work like stylesheets. One source of truth, cascading logic, inheritance where sensible. They do not. They work like a bureaucracy that accumulated rules over fifteen years without anyone auditing whether the rules still made sense.

The project lost four hours last week to a view template override nobody remembered setting. I am told this is normal.
