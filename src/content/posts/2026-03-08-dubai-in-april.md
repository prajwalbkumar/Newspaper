<span class="dateline">DUBAI —</span> It is not the number. The number means nothing. You see 42°C on your phone and process it abstractly. The sensation arrives when you open the car door and the air inside is a different substance than air.

By May it will be worse. By June the city conducts most of its life at night or indoors. You get used to it in the way you get used to anything that has no alternative.
