The flight was missed by eleven minutes. The next one was six hours later. The Hamad International Airport has a large bear sculpture and very good coffee at 3am. Six hours is long enough to read one hundred and twenty pages of a book you were meaning to read. It is not quite long enough to stop being annoyed about the eleven minutes.
