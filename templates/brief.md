**Opening label —** The brief has no visible headline on the card, so the first sentence carries the full weight. Lead with the label in bold — "Two hours on a Thursday —", "IronPython, again —", "The meeting ran long —" — then get straight to the point.

Keep it short. A brief is under 100 words. It's an observation, a finding, a small truth. If it's growing past two paragraphs, it wants to be a story.
